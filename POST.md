# LinkedIn Post - Quick Version

**Tired of vetting recruiter messages?**

Got a DM from a "recruiter at Amazon"? Before you reply, ask yourself:
- Does their email actually match the company domain?
- Is their LinkedIn profile brand new?
- Does the job actually exist on their careers page?

Modern scammers are good at this. Really good.

I built a tool that checks all of this automatically in seconds—no detective work required. Just ask Claude to verify the opportunity and you're done.

If you've been hit with a fake recruiter message, you're not alone. If you're tired of the manual vetting, check out the **LinkedIn-Scam-Detector** skill.
